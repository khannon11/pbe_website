Overlooking the Charles River, Back Bay, downtown Boston, and Fenway Park, Phi Beta Epsilon stands in the hotspot of the MIT campus, which means you're never far away from anything! Within the five floors of Phi Beta Epsilon, you'll discover a house of fun and relaxation, awesome amenities, and all in all, great space to do whatever you want.

Each room in Phi Beta Epsilon comes furnished, and there are bathroom facilities on every floor. We have a mix of triples and singles, so it's easy to find a room that fits your style.

Besides the individual rooms, we have:

  • Fully-equipped kitchen and dining room
  • Meals provided 2x a day by our renowned chef, John Peredina
  • Bar and lounge area with a beautiful antique pool table
  • The Spiker entertainment room for our HDTV projector and 120" screen
  • Spacious living room with vintage Steinway & Sons grand piano
  • Two complete sets of DJ turn tables
  • Foosball table
  • Fraternity parking lot
  • Laundry facilities
  • Study room and library
  • Storage on every floor

PBE's signature floor is our roofdeck, renowned for its stellar environment and breathtaking views.

Although our house is awesome now, there's always room for improvement. With the support of hundreds of Phi Beta Epsilon alumni, we are about to undertake a massive renovation to make our house even better.
